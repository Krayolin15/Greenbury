Admissions Process (Grade 8 for 2026)

Applications for Greenbury Secondary School are generally handled **directly at the school** rather than exclusively via the provincial online system. We advise parents to visit the school during the admission period, typically **May to July** of the preceding year (e.g., May‚ÄìJuly 2025 for 2026 Grade 8 entry).

Required Documents:

  • Learner's **Birth Certificate**
  • Parent/Guardian **ID Copies**
  • **Proof of Residence**
  • Learner's **Grade 7 Latest Report**

For guidance, please contact our Provincial Learner Admissions Coordinators or the school office.

School Fees

Greenbury Secondary is a **public, fee-paying school** classified as **Quintile 4**. Fees cover operational expenses and are lower than private schools due to partial government funding (approx. R803 per learner subsidy).
